Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

This is a demonstration website. We do not collect or store any personal information on remote servers. All data you enter (such as login credentials, form submissions, and problem ratings) is stored locally on your device using your browser's LocalStorage API.

2. Data Usage

The data stored in your browser is used solely to simulate the functionality of a dynamic website, such as remembering your login state or displaying your submitted problems in the admin dashboard.

3. Data Persistence

Since data is stored locally, it will persist until you clear your browser's cache or local storage data. We cannot recover any data if you clear your browser history or use a different device.

4. Third-Party Services

This website uses Google Fonts and may inadvertently make requests to third-party CDNs for assets like images or fonts. These requests are subject to the respective privacy policies of those providers.

5. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Technical "cookies" (LocalStorage) are used strictly for functional purposes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us via our Contact Page.
