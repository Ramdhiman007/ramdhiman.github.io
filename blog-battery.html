Windows Laptop Battery Draining Fast – 10 Ways to Extend Life

Practical steps to squeeze more hours from your laptop battery.

1) Check Battery Health

Generate a report to see capacity wear.

How? PowerShell (Admin): powercfg /batteryreport → open report HTML.

2) Use Battery Saver and Balanced Plan

Reduces background activity and screen brightness.

3) Tame Background Apps

Settings → Apps → Startup; disable unnecessary apps. Close heavy apps when on battery.

4) Optimize Video and Display

Lower brightness; enable video playback optimization in Settings → Apps → Video playback.

5) Update Drivers and BIOS

OEM power/thermal fixes often ship via BIOS/driver updates.

6) Disable Unused Radios

Turn off Bluetooth and Wi‑Fi when not needed; prefer Airplane mode.

7) Use Edge Efficiency Mode

Browsers can drain battery; enable Efficiency mode in Edge.

8) Replace Aging Battery

If design capacity loss is high (>25%), consider a replacement.
